5. Pro Activation and License Verification

ChroFlow Pro requires license activation.

When you enter a Lemon Squeezy license key in ChroFlow, the app may send the license key and limited device-specific activation information to our verification server. This is necessary to verify the license and create a local signed license file for your device.

Our verification server is used only as a relay for the license verification process. It forwards the relevant activation request to Lemon Squeezy or another authorized licensing service and returns the activation result or local signed license file to your device.

Our verification server does not collect, store, read, or analyze your notes, tasks, timers, timelines, categories, sessions, productivity records, or other ChroFlow content.

Our verification server does not retain your device-specific activation information after the verification process is completed.
Depending on how license activation is configured, Lemon Squeezy or another authorized licensing provider may process purchase, payment, tax, invoice, license key, activation status, activation count, activation instance, device management, refund, and related account information according to its own terms and privacy policy.
